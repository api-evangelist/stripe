Visa's Digital Commerce Authentication Program (DCAP) is a new global framework that reduces fraud and increases authorization rates for card-not-present transactions, rewarding US businesses with a net interchange reduction of five basis points for sharing richer transaction data during authentication. Stripe moved quickly to help its businesses take advantage of DCAP, capturing interchange savings while protecting authorization rates.
